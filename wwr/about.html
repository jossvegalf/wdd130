History

In 1985, the concept of Whitewater Rafting was born with the purpose of creating unforgettable experiences for brave souls while sharing our deep passion for rafting with a wider audience but it didn't take long for a wave of adventure enthusiasts to find their way to us, all eager for new challenges and full of adrenaline. Fast forward to today, we have earned a well-deserved reputation as one of the world's leading international adventure companies. With this web service our clients can conveniently book their trips online, access detailed information about our rafting adventures and even share their impressive photographs and valuable comments for other rafting enthusiasts.
